About

The Sound of Art are concerts of film and video game music hold all around Europe.

You can look forward to our own orchestra, that is consisting of 95 members, and a stunning light show. In addition, you can watch edited montages from the films, TV shows and video games while listening to the live performance.

Our program is consisting of the newest soundtracks as well as timeless classics. The programs are specially crafted to offer wide range of genres and styles. You can look forward to soundtracks from renowned composers like Hans Zimmer, Ennio Morricone or Ramin Djawadi.
